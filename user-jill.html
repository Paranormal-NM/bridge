Paranormal-NM

"Jill"

by Duncan Colhoun


Jill's Persona

Name: Jill Parker

Technology: iPad, iPhone, networked PC running W10 and MS Office

Proficiency: Jill is a bank teller. She requires few IT skills beyond using the bank's accounting app and Microsoft Word. She has average skills using her phone and tablet, which she sees as means to an end, not as an end (hobby/business) in themselves. She spends a lot of time texting, and about once a week she uses Quicken on her iPad to update her financial records.

Passion for Technology: Very little. Jill spends most of her free time reading printed books and watching movies on television.

Attitudes and Needs: Jill likes reading printed novels and watching movies on TV. She is very interested in the paranormal. She watches a variety of ghost hunting shows on weekends. She believes in the existence of ghosts.

Frustrations: Jill is frustrated trying to raise 10 year old Freddy. He spends too much time playing with his imaginary (?) friend, Darryl. Sometime Freddy tells her things that happened on her job, or something that is going to happen - and soon does! Freddy says he gets his info from Darryl. Jill wonders if Darryl is a ghost. She wishes she could learn more about ghosts and their interactions with children.

Role: Topic-focused user

User Story

As a topic-focused user, I want to use Paranormal-NM to validate my suspicion that my son's companion is the ghost of a young boy.

User Interaction

A possible interaction between Jill and Paranormal-NM might go like this:

Precondition: Jill wants to validate her suspicions about Darryl being a ghost.

  • Jill googles "ghosts of children." Google sends a list of possible links.
  • Jill selects "Paranormal-NM" from the search list. Paranormal-NM loads the landing page.
  • Jill is pulled into the site by the content of the landing page. She clicks the icon for the Ghosts category The site loads the list of stories in the database onto the Ghose.
  • After browsing the titles of user-contributed ghost stories, she clicks on several story links. The server loads each story onto a page with the category name. After reading each story, Jill returns to the category page. This process repeats several times.
  • Jill clicks the "Home" link. The site reloads the landing page and bookmarks the site.

Post-experience: Jill has tentatively validated her suspicion. She will continue visiting Paranormal-NM.
